A gryphon appears before you, surrounded by a shining aura. It
has the head of a falcon, from which long, narrow ears protrude.
Its body is covered by feathers that are so dark blue, that they
appear almost black. Behind its shoulders huge wings protrude
from its body, which are in no way inferior to those of a dragon.
However these resemble more those of an eagle than those of
a dragon. Above the yellow beak sits a pair of shining green
eyes with black pupils, which fixate you.
